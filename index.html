Dara Rubin | Designer | Chicago, Ill.

I graduated from Northwestern University in March 2017 with a Journalism degree from the Medill School of Journalism and a Design Certificate from the Segal Design Institute. Journalism taught me how to write and communicate well, and design taught me to think outside the box and not be afraid to share my ideas.

I am currently working at Scranton Gillette Communications in Arlington Heights, Ill. as the Creative Services Coordinator.

Outside of work, I love hiking, watching and playing sports, going to concerts, and figuring out how to get people on the street to stop and let me pet their dog.
